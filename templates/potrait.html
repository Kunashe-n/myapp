{{ 'Title: Kunyeyana' }}

Medium: Mixed Art Media(Oils and Braids)

Year: 2025

Size: 84cm x 60cm

Price: USD$500

Status: Available for purchase

"Kunyeyana," a captivating visual narrative, invites viewers to immerse themselves in the intricate dynamics of human interaction. This mixed-media masterpiece, is a poignant exploration of the age-old phenomenon of gossiping. At its core, "Kunyeyana" is an astute observation of the ways in which individuals engage with one another, often veiling their true intentions behind a mask of camaraderie. The artwork presents a tableau of figures, their facial expressions and body language conveying a sense of intrigue, curiosity, and subtle malice. Through the artist's deliberate choice of colors, textures, and composition, the viewer is drawn into a world of whispered secrets, furtive glances, and surreptitious smiles. The dominant hues of blues and greens evoke a sense of melancholy and unease, underscoring the notion that gossiping can be a corrosive and damaging force in our relationships.
