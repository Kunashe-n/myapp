About Willard Magiga

Willard Magiga is a visual artist born in Chivi rural District, Masvingo in 2002. He discovered his talent at the young age of nine. During high school, he discovered his passion for custom portraits. Taking commissions and selling his artwork allowed him to earn extra money for school expenses. This experience taught him the value of turning his passion into a meaningful pursuit. He became a full-time artist soon after finishing his Ordinary Level in 2021. His work is inspired by African culture and wildlife. Willard Magiga believes in giving back to the community.
